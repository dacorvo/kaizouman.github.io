Why the name Kaizou ?

Kaizō(改造) is a chinese and japanese word that can be translated into English as "Modded" or "Hacked".

Kaizou is the closest phonetic equivalent in french to the japanese pronunciation.

I acquired the domain name to develop a mozilla extension that allowed the modification of web pages, and although the extension is no longer maintained, I kept the name ...
